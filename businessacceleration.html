Business Acceleration Programme

Empowering local entrepreneurs and enterprises to scale efficiently through strategic growth, innovation, and market readiness support.

Programme Overview

The Business Acceleration Programme is designed to help emerging and established enterprises fast-track their growth. Pulse Movement provides mentorship, funding access, digital transformation tools, and market expansion opportunities.

Core Objectives

  • Support SMEs with high-growth potential in scaling operations.
  • Enhance access to finance, technology, and business networks.
  • Facilitate strategic partnerships and market linkages.
  • Promote innovation-driven business models.
  • Strengthen local value chains and inclusive economic participation.

Key Components

  • 1. Business Diagnostics & Strategy: In-depth analysis to identify barriers to growth and develop actionable plans.
  • 2. Mentorship & Coaching: Access to seasoned mentors, industry experts, and business coaches.
  • 3. Access to Finance: Linking entrepreneurs to investors, grant programs, and financial institutions.
  • 4. Market Expansion: Creating market access platforms and business-to-business matchmaking.
  • 5. Digital Enablement: Helping businesses integrate digital tools for efficiency and scalability.

Who Can Apply?

  • Small and medium enterprises (SMEs) with at least 2 years of operation.
  • Entrepreneurs seeking to expand production, market share, or exports.
  • Innovative startups with scalable business models.
