Our Business Facilitation Mission

The Pulse Movement drives sustainable business growth by facilitating partnerships between entrepreneurs, industries, and government. We provide mentorship, advisory services, and development programs that strengthen business ecosystems and unlock new market opportunities.

Enterprise Development

We support start-ups and established enterprises through mentorship programs, entrepreneurial clinics, and supplier development strategies.

Business Advisory

Our experts provide business profiling, coaching, and market access facilitation to help local entrepreneurs grow sustainably.

Market Development

We build value chains and create new trade pipelines that connect small businesses to regional, national, and international markets.

Programme Outcomes

  • Local Business Acceleration initiatives improving enterprise performance.
  • Public-private partnerships that unlock funding and mentorship opportunities.
  • Entrepreneurial skills development through monthly SMME fairs and business symposia.
  • Increased market visibility for local suppliers and service providers.
