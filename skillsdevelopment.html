Skills Development

Building practical, market-relevant skills for youth and adults so they can access opportunities, start businesses and participate meaningfully in the local green and digital economies.

Our Mission in Skills Development

To equip individuals and communities with practical and future-ready skills — through blended training, mentorship, and business-linked interventions — so talent can move seamlessly into employment, entrepreneurship and leadership roles.

Core Training Areas

Digital & 4IR Skills

AI fundamentals, basic coding, robotics introduction, data literacy, and digital tools for business.

Vocational & TVET

Practical trades, green skills, manufacturing basics and hands-on technical training linked to industry.

Entrepreneurship & Business

Business planning, market access, finance-readiness, enterprise profiling and incubation support.

Delivery Model

Blended Learning

Combines in-person workshops and community clinics with online modules and remote mentoring to maximize reach and accessibility.

Work Integrated Learning

Placement and coaching in local businesses (WIL) to convert skills into workplace experience and improved hiring outcomes.

Programme Outcomes

  • • Improved employability & placement rates for graduates of our programmes.
  • • Entrepreneurship pipelines established via bursary, incubation and market linkages.
  • • Localised talent pools aligned to regional industry needs (green, tech, creative).
  • • Continuous monitoring & evaluation enabling just-in-time improvements.
