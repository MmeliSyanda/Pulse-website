Who We Are

The Pulse Movement

Pulse Movement (268-505 NPO) is a community-driven organization dedicated to fostering economic inclusion, skills development, and local enterprise growth through the 360¬∫ Local Economic Development Support Programme (360¬∫ LEDSP). We bridge the gap between talent, opportunity, and growth by connecting communities, businesses, and government to create sustainable change.
